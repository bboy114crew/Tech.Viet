GOCOM.VN A WEB PLATFORM FOR WHOLESALE BUSINESSES IN VIETNAM

PROBLEMS

? Vietnamese wholesale buyers encounter much difficulties in searching for products, services and contacts of small and medium wholesalers, because:

? To buyers, sellers? information from sources such as search engines or social networks is not sufficiently complete and reliable; and

To sellers, promoting their products and services is costly and complicated due to their limited resources of finance, human and technology.

THE SOLUTION

? A web platform where small and medium wholesalers can promote information about their products and services and where wholesale buyers can search for such information.

? To buyers, search results are:

? More complete since GOCOM.VN is a community of a large number of sellers; and

? More reliable since sellers are verified, rated and reviewed.

? To sellers, promoting products and services is:

? Cheaper thanks to affordable online marketing services such as web page development and maintenance; and

? Easier thanks to user-friendly integrated online marketing tools such as mass emails.
