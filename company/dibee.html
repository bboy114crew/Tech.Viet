About

Creating A Foundation For Growth

With 20 years of experience in the corporate services to improve productivity in Asia, we recognized the Vietnamese economy was growing rapidly and would need a technological solution to help support the increasingly crucial logistics industry.

So, Dibee created a solution that redefines how you can control fuel expenses, reduce costs and optimize fleet management, all within a secure and transparent system. However, it is more than just a system for securely managing transactions, it is also part of an overall transformative strategy.

Digital solutions are the key to enabling businesses to not only increase efficiency and productivity, but also to unlock new opportunities for growth and entirely new avenues of commerce through easier integration with clients and partners.

Dibee was founded to address the growing pains of managing fleet fuel expenses in just a few clicks. It provides a platform to handle this complex and time-consuming process in the fastest, easiest and most efficient way possible.

The kind of stringent, transparent record management provided by Dibee is also the basis for building reputational assets, which allows companies to more easily access the financial support that enables expansion.

Indeed, this is the very foundation for growth across entire industries and the nation's economy as a whole. At Dibee, we are proud to be playing our part in the digital transformation of Vietnam.
