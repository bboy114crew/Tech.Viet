About

iVIVU.com is Vietnam's first online hotel & tour booking website that offers the most innovative online solution for finding & booking hotels rooms with instant confirmation system, convenient payment methods and exceptional customer service. iVIVU.com has an inventory of more than 2,000 hotels in Vietnam and 30,000 internationally, in addition to 200 tour products in Vietnam and Southeast Asia, serving primarily domestic customers.

iVIVU.com is a subsidiary of TMG Vietnam (http://www.thienminhtravel.com/), Asia's leading travel & hospitality group based in Vietnam.
